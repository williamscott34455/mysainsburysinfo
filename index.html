Hidden Features of the MySainsburys Portal: How Employees Can Get the Most Out of Their Dashboard

The MySainsburys portal has become an essential digital workspace for thousands of Sainsbury’s employees across the UK. It’s commonly known as a place to check payslips, view schedules, and manage HR information - but that’s only scratching the surface. https://mysainsburysinfo.com/

What many employees don’t realize is that the platform includes a range of hidden features and productivity tools that can make everyday work easier, faster, and even more rewarding. Whether you’re a new team member or a long-time employee, learning these insider tips can help you unlock the true power of MySainsburys.

1. Customizing Your Dashboard for Efficiency

When you log into the MySainsburys employee portal, you’re greeted by a default dashboard showing announcements, payslip links, and quick navigation icons. However, few employees realize that this page is customizable.

You can rearrange modules, pin your most-used shortcuts (like “Payslips,” “Shift Planner,” or “Learning”), and even hide unused panels. This feature helps you cut down navigation time - especially useful if you access the portal daily from a store computer or mobile device.

Tip: Hover over a section header and look for the small “pin” or “settings” icon - that’s where you can personalize your dashboard layout.

2. Downloading Historical Payslips and P60s

Most staff members only view their latest payslip online. But did you know MySainsburys keeps years of archived payslips and P60 documents?

By navigating to the Payroll > Payslip Archive section, you can securely download all your past records for tax or mortgage purposes. The documents are stored as encrypted PDFs - a safer alternative to paper versions that can be lost or damaged.

This feature saves employees the hassle of contacting HR for older records and ensures you always have your payment history available, even if you change departments.

3. Accessing the “MyLearning” Platform Without Leaving the Portal

The MySainsburys site isn’t just about admin - it’s also your gateway to professional growth. Hidden within the top navigation bar is a link called MyLearning, which redirects you to Sainsbury’s internal training hub.

Here, employees can access:

  • Product knowledge modules
  • Health and safety training
  • Customer service improvement courses
  • Leadership development resources

Many employees overlook this area because it’s not prominently displayed. Yet completing these courses can increase your skill set and may even influence promotion opportunities.

4. Shift Alerts and Notifications Setup

Managing shifts is one of the main reasons employees log into MySainsburys. But did you know you can enable automatic notifications for schedule changes?

Under Settings > Notifications, you can activate:

  • Email alerts when your rota changes
  • SMS reminders for upcoming shifts
  • Push notifications (if you use the mobile version)

This feature ensures you’re never caught off guard by last-minute scheduling updates - a lifesaver for staff working varied shifts or across multiple locations.

5. Real-Time HR Queries and Support Requests

Most employees still email HR for help. But the portal has an integrated “Ask HR” tool that allows you to raise tickets directly through the system.

Once submitted, you’ll receive a tracking ID and can monitor your case status. This not only saves time but also ensures your query reaches the right department.

Example requests include:

  • Payroll corrections
  • Time-off requests
  • Pension or tax information updates

You’ll find this tool under Employee Services > HR Queries.

6. Exclusive Discounts and Employee Offers Hub

While most people know about general Sainsbury’s staff discounts, the portal hosts an exclusive offers section often missed by employees.

By selecting MyBenefits from the dashboard, you can access rotating deals on:

  • Gym memberships
  • Travel and holidays
  • Technology products
  • Retail partners like Argos and Habitat

These discounts change monthly, so checking the section regularly can save you significant money outside of work.

7. Performance Feedback and Self-Assessment Tools

Hidden under the MyDevelopment tab is a feature designed for performance management. It allows employees to:

  • Set personal development goals
  • Record achievements
  • Request digital feedback from line managers

This data feeds into Sainsbury’s annual review system, helping both employees and managers track growth objectively.

8. Accessing Canteen Menus and Local Announcements

Not widely advertised, the portal includes a “Store Info” section for specific locations. It displays:

  • Canteen menus for the week
  • Local staff announcements
  • Store-level policy updates

9. Multi-Device Compatibility - Using MySainsburys on Mobile

MySainsburys isn’t just desktop-friendly. The portal’s responsive design means you can access it on your smartphone or tablet without needing an app.

Pro Tip: Clear cookies occasionally for smoother performance and fewer login timeouts.

10. Using the “Knowledge Base” for Self-Help

One of the best-kept secrets is the built-in Knowledge Base - a searchable help centre containing official guides, policy documents, and step-by-step instructions.

Before reaching out to HR, try searching for keywords like “holiday entitlement,” “sick pay,” or “data protection.” In many cases, you’ll find instant answers verified by the HR department itself.

11. Managing Work-Life Balance with Leave Planning Tools

Under the Time & Attendance section, you can find tools for requesting holidays and tracking leave balances. What’s lesser-known is that the portal allows you to view your team’s leave calendar, helping avoid overlaps when planning vacations.

12. Data Privacy and Security Options

Your personal data on MySainsburys is encrypted, but employees can add extra security layers manually. By enabling two-step verification and setting up security questions, you make unauthorized access virtually impossible.
